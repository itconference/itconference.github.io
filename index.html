This service has been developed and maintained by the independent community of software developers from Sweden and Berlin. We believe that too many conferences claim to be good, but it's far from the truth.

Our opinionated ranking formula is based on multiple factors. The factors include, but are not limited to: verified social network reviews (google, facebook, trustpilot, blog posts), sentiment analysis, what attendees say to us, customer service, price vs. quality ratio, our expert opinion (if we attended the conference).

We'd also like to thank amazing people who invest time in writing feedback and online reviews. Thanks to you, services like this can emerge.
